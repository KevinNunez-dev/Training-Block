Runner's Tips

Hydration

Stay Hydrated

Drink 16-20 oz of water 2 hrs before every run. Carry water or use stations on runs over 6 miles.

Fuel

Fuel & Nutrition

Eat a light carb snack 45-60 min pre-run. Use energy gels or chews on runs longer than 60 minutes.

Recovery

Sleep & Recovery

Aim for 7-9 hrs of sleep. Schedule rest days seriously — they build fitness just as runs do.

Gear

Gear Check

Rotate shoes every 300-500 miles. Moisture-wicking socks prevent blisters on long runs.

Pace

Easy Pace Rule

80% of your runs should feel conversational. Save speed work for designated tempo days only.

Warm-Up

Warm-Up & Cool-Down

5-min brisk walk before every run. 5-min walk + 10-min stretch after every run to reduce soreness.
