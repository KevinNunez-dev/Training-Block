Runner's Tips

Hydration

Stay Hydrated

Drink 16-20 oz of water 2 hrs before every run. Carry water or use a belt on runs over 6 miles.

Fuel

Fuel & Nutrition

Light carb snack 45-60 min before runs. Use energy gels on long runs over 60 minutes.

Recovery

Sleep & Recovery

Aim for 7-9 hrs of sleep. Sunday recovery runs should be truly easy — no ego pace.

Yoga

Monday Yoga

Monday yoga is your reset. Focus on hip flexors, hamstrings, and calves — the runner's trifecta.

Track

Track Days

Track days build speed and form. Warm up before hitting your workout intervals or threshold efforts.

Long Run

Saturday Long Run

Run Saturdays at a conversational pace — 60-90 sec/mile slower than your goal race pace.
